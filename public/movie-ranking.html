The Best Movies According to D'Angelo

My top Movies of All Time

Everything Everywhere All at Once

At surface level, it looks like a mundane silly movie, but what I love the most about is the optimistic nihilism message that it carries deep down.

The Pursuit of Happiness

Life will beat you down, it will do it again, and again, and again. Yet, despite that we must keep standing up again, and again because eventually, we will strike back.

Hustle

Adam Sandler at his best, a film that takes the blind fold off of your eyes and makes you remember that hard work beats talent, every time.
